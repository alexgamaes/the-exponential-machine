# Timeline of 50 Pivotal Events (1939-1945)

This timeline represents the high-velocity expansion of **The Exponential Machine** at Bletchley Park. These events are categorized as Mathematical, Hardware, Organizational, or Intelligence milestones.

## 1939: The Genesis Load
1.  **Aug 15:** Arrival at Bletchley Park. The "Dirty Dozen" establish the first logic gates in flesh.
2.  **Sept 4:** Alan Turing joins GC&CS. The Central Processing Unit is initialized.
3.  **Oct 21:** Receipt of the Polish Zygalski Sheets. A manual search-reduction boost.
4.  **Nov 12:** Formation of Hut 6 (Army/Air Enigma). Logic sector 1 initialized.
5.  **Dec 15:** Formation of Hut 8 (Naval Enigma). Logic sector 2 initialized.
6.  **Dec 30:** Recruitment of the First 50 "Computers." Parallel processing capacity +50.

## 1940: The Architecture of Contradiction
7.  **Jan 6:** First manual break of German Army Enigma. Proof of concept for human-meat calculation.
8.  **Feb 12:** The "Herivel Tip" discovered. A heuristic exploit based on operator laziness.
9.  **Mar 14:** Installation of "Victory" (Agnus Dei). The first electromechanical Bombe (1,000 OPS).
10. **Apr 22:** Welchman’s "Diagonal Board" proposal. A 100x hardware efficiency multiplier.
11. **May 10:** Creation of Hut 3. Intelligence output buffering and translation layer.
12. **June 1:** Recruitment of the first Wrens. Industrial-scale operator scaling begins.
13. **July 20:** The "Cille" Exploit discovered. A pattern-recognition algorithm for rotor-start positions.
14. **Aug 8:** Installation of "Agnus Dei" with Diagonal Board. Throughput hits 17,000 states/min.
15. **Sept 15:** First naval Enigma break (Off-line). Proof of Naval state-space susceptibility.

## 1941: The Industrialization of Search
16. **Apr 21:** Capture of the Krebs weather ship codebooks. "Data Pinch" provides massive decryption boost.
17. **Mar 1:** Turing invents "Banburismus." A mathematical technique to reduce the Enigma search space.
18. **May 9:** Capture of U-110 (The "Pinch"). Total Naval Enigma logic mapping achieved.
19. **June 14:** Breaking the German "Railway Enigma." Supply-chain data unlock.
20. **Aug 22:** Opening of the "Eastcote" Outstation. Scaling hardware beyond the central campus.
21. **Oct 21:** "Action This Day" Memo. Churchill grants "Extreme Priority" budget and resources.
22. **Nov 12:** Establishment of the "Card Index." A high-capacity, manual memory bus for 3 million records.
23. **Dec 20:** First breaks of the Abwehr (Spy) Enigma. High-level counter-intelligence unlock.

## 1942: The Blackout and the Lorenz Frontier
24. **Feb 1:** Introduction of 4-rotor Enigma (M4). Naval search space increases by 26x. **The Blackout begins.**
25. **Mar 10:** Turing invents "Turingery." A manual method for breaking the Lorenz (Tunny) teletype cipher.
26. **May 15:** Establishment of the "Newmanry." A dedicated sector for mechanized teletype decryption.
27. **July 1:** The US/UK BRUSA Agreement. Global parallel-processing collaboration.
28. **Aug 18:** First prototype of "Heath Robinson." An optical/tape reader for Lorenz. High-speed IO attempt.
29. **Sept 22:** Breaking of the "Sturgeon" (German teletype). Intelligence yield +200%.
30. **Oct 30:** Capture of U-559 codebooks. The key to ending the Naval Blackout.
31. **Dec 13:** The 4-rotor Enigma (Shark) is broken. The Naval Blackout ends.

## 1943: The Rise of the Electronic Ghost
32. **Feb 1:** Move to Block D. Transition from wooden huts to purpose-built industrial logic blocks.
33. **Mar 14:** Installation of the 100th Bombe. System throughput hits 2,000,000 OPS.
34. **Apr 22:** Tommy Flowers proposes "Colossus." The shift from relays to vacuum tubes.
35. **June 1:** Breaking the "Tunny" (Lorenz) cipher at scale. High-command intelligence unlock.
36. **Aug 15:** The "Stanmore" outstation becomes operational. Global hardware scaling peak.
37. **Oct 10:** Arrival of the US Navy Bombes in Dayton, Ohio. Trans-Atlantic load balancing.
38. **Dec 8:** First Colossus Mark I test. The "Electron" replaces the "Relay" (5,000 characters/sec).

## 1944: The Parallel Revolution
39. **Feb 5:** Colossus Mark I becomes fully operational. The world's first programmable digital computer.
40. **Mar 1:** Breaking the "Hungarian Enigma." Expansion of the search scope to Axis satellites.
41. **May 22:** Preparation for D-Day (Operation Overlord). Pre-processing 100% of West-Wall traffic.
42. **June 1:** Colossus Mark II arrives. 5x parallel processing speed.
43. **July 12:** Expansion of "The Beast." The Naval Card Index reaches industrial scale under Pamela Rose.
44. **Aug 20:** Breaking the "Japanese Purple" cipher (The B-Ring breakthrough).
45. **Oct 15:** Implementation of "The Grid." Synchronized global data-routing for intercept stations.

## 1945: The Great Erase
46. **Jan 1:** Peak Workforce: 10,000 personnel. The Compute hits its biological limit.
47. **Mar 14:** Tenth Colossus Mark II installed. The peak of the electronic search engine.
48. **May 8:** VE Day. The Enigma search space collapses to zero.
49. **May 22:** The Great Decommissioning. Churchill orders the destruction of the 211 Bombes and 10 Colossi to preserve the "Ultra" secret.
50. **June 30:** The Dispersal. The "Compute" goes underground. The beginning of the Cold War era.
